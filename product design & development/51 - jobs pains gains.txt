# 51. What is a customer profile model? How can they be used to improve design. [9]

customer profile model has
    jobs:
        what the customer wants to get done
        functional - to complete a task
        social - gain status or power
        emotional - achieve an emotional state
    pains:
        negative that the customer experiences or could experience
    gains:
        benefits to the customer that are expected, desired, or are surprising

product propositions which seek to address these three things

product and services which complete customer jobs
gain creators, address gains
pain relievers, cope with negative aspects
