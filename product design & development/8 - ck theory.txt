# 8. Explain how design is a learning process. [8]

knowledge space
- stuff you know to be true or false

concept space
- stuff you don't know to be true or false

K-K 
expansion by deduction

K-C
combine knowledge to make concepts

C-C
expand concepts by creativity

C-K
validation by experiment

K is unordered
C is a progression tree

Design is terminated with C >> K
