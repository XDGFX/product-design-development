# 20. Define pollution, it's types, and the most common sources. [3, 11 (most common)]

pollution is :
    the introduction of physical (e.g. radiation, chemical substances)
    or biological (e.g. manure, seeds) agents into an ecosystem to an amount it cannot
    be absorbed, hence leading to its adverse disruption 

types of pollution are:
    natural / man made
    accidental / chronic
    local / dispersed

most common sources are: 
    eutrophication
    acid rain
    ozone hole
    smog
    global warming
    light pollution
    land use
