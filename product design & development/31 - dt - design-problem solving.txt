# 31. Design is a learning process. Discuss. [12]

design is not problem solving, but an exploration process to reduce obscurity

C-K theory

Parralel expansion of C concept space (unknown) and K knowledge space (true or false)
- Generation of concepts by knowledge conbination K - C
- Expansion of concepts by creativity C - C
- Expansion of knowledge by deduction K - K
- Concept validation by experimenation and prototyping C - K

Design terminates when concept turns into knowledge

Concept is a tree where the root is an initial concept
Knowledge is an unordered list
