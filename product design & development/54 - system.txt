# 54. Define and give examples of ‘system’ and ‘system design’ and ‘system complexity’. [6]

system:
    regularly interactive independent group of items forming a unified hole

system design:
    process of defining architecture, modules, interfaces, and data for a system to satisfy requirements

system complexity:
    number of parts
    number of types of parts.
    number of inter connections
    number of functions that the product is expected to perform
    OR number of inter-dependencies and constraints.
